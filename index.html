About IET VVit

The Institution of Engineering and Technology (IET) at Vasireddy Venkatadri Institute of Technology is a prestigious student chapter that serves as a bridge between academia and industry. Our mission is to inspire, inform and influence the global engineering community to engineer a better world.

With a legacy of excellence and innovation, IET VVit provides students with opportunities to develop technical skills, leadership abilities, and professional networks that are essential for success in the engineering field.
